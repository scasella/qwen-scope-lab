The steering field

Two ways to push a model's hidden state from one concept value to another. The teal comet is manifold steering — it rides the fitted ring through activation space, and at every waypoint the burst is the real token the model produced there. The salmon comet is the linear chord — it cuts across the ring's empty interior and snaps to the nearest endpoint. Pick a ring, hit launch.
