Spot jailbreaks inside your own model — for free.

Paste a message your chatbot might receive. A tiny detector reads the model's own internal activity and flags manipulation attempts in real time.

No second AI, no API call: the verdict is one dot product on activations the model already computes. Below, you can compare it to a paid AI judge on the same message.

Honest note. This runs on a small 2-billion-parameter research model, and the alarm threshold is calibrated on a small sample. The detector's judgment is strong, but in a real deployment you would re-tune the threshold on your own traffic — that's the one piece that isn't plug-and-play.
The detector is a difference-of-means linear probe over the model's residual stream; the paid judge is OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini. Built on the Qwen Scope Lab Bench.
